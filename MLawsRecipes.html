One-pan Salmon and Broccoli





Ingredients

  • 4 small salmon steaks
  • 2 heads of broccoli, cut into florets
  • 4 tbsp soy sauce, divided
  • 2 tbsp rice vinegar, divided
  • 2 tbsp sesame oil, divided
  • oil for greasing pan


Instructions

  1. Preheat oven for 425F // grease a large baking sheet with oil.
  2. In a large dish, whisk whisk 2 tbsp soy sauce, 1 tbsp rice vinegar and 1 tbsp sesame oil. Add salmon and marinate for 15 minutes.
  3. Meanwhile, in a large bowl whisk remaining soy sauce, vinegar and oil. Toss with broccoli and arrange on the baking sheet. Arrange salmon on sheet.
  4. Roast for 20-25 minutes or until cooked through.

Creamy Spiced Acorn Squash Soup





Ingredients

  • 2 acorn squash
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3/4 tsp salt
  • 1.2 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1/4 tsp allspice
  • 1/4 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/8 tsp nutmeg
  • 1/8 tsp cayenne pepper (optional)
  • 5 cups water
  • 1/4 cup 35% cream

Instructions

  1. Prepare squash, poke all over with a fork and microwave about 4-5 minutes. Allow to cool and scoop out flesh.
  2. Heat butter over medium-low heat in a large pot. Add onion and cook until softened, about 5 minutes. Add spices and salt, cook for 30 seconds.
  3. Add squash and water. Bring to a boil. Cover, reduce heat to low and simmer for 10-12 minutes. Puree with a blender.
  4. Stir in cream and serve.

Grilled Eggplant Skewers





Ingredients

  • 18-20 skewers, soaked in water for 20 minutes
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 tsp chili flakes
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 eggplant, cut into 1-inch cubes


Instructions

  1. Preheat grill to medium heat.
  2. Whisk olive oil with garlic, chili flakes and salt.
  3. Toss with eggplant, and skewer.
  4. Grill for 1-2 minutes per side or until grill marks appear.
  5. Serve immediately.
